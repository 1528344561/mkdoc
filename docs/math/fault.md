一个错误的解法:
$$
\begin{aligned}
&sin(\sqrt{(4n^2+n)}\pi)\\
&= sin(\sqrt{(4n^2+n)}\pi - 2n\pi) \\
&= \sqrt{(4n^2+n)}\pi - 2n\pi \\
&= 2n\pi(\sqrt{1+(1/4n)} - 1) \\
&= 2n\pi*(1/8n) \\
&= \pi/4
\end{aligned}
$$
